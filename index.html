About Me

Maggie Bermudez

Your Personal Trainer

Maggie Bermudez is a powerhouse personal trainer with over six years of experience, currently the top revenue-producing trainer at the largest health club in Bucks County, PA. Maggie brings dedication, resilience, and a contagious passion for fitness and well-being that inspires everyone she works with. A proud wife, mother to two adult daughters, and dog mom to two French Bulldogs, she has transformed her passion for fitness into a purpose-driven career, empowering countless clients to reach their goals and build lasting healthy habits.

Beyond her success as a personal trainer, Maggie embraces every opportunity to challenge herself, including her recent leap into the world of women's tackle football with the Women's National Football Conference (WNFC). As a defensive back, she not only made the team but embodied her favorite mantra: "The same voice that says, 'give up' can also be trained to say, 'keep going.'"

In every role she takes on—from trainer to teammate to community builder—Maggie thrives on creating spaces where people come together, stay motivated, and support one another. Her goal is to help others find their strength and tenacity, not only in fitness but in life.
